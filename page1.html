Full Set (Engagement & Usage)

It's 6:00 PM. Sophie's peak gym time.

Checking the App

Before heading out, she checks the PureGym app to see how busy it is. "Very Busy (78%)" flashes on her screen, but she goes anyway, it's when she always trains.

Squat Rack Wait

She enters using her PIN, waves to the staff member by the desk, and heads straight to the squat rack. Except, all three racks are taken. Two people are chatting between sets, another's scrolling through their phone.

Improvising Her Workout

She improvises, uses the Smith machine instead. Later, she notices a few weights lying around, a couple of broken resistance cables, and overhears the same playlist loop again.

Familiar Rhythm

Still, she feels at home. She's used to this rhythm, the buzz, the regular faces, the unspoken routine.

Muscle Aches (Pain Points)

  • Overcrowding during peak hours.

  • Sophie has built her own flow and routine.

Mindset Check
