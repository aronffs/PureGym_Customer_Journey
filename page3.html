Warm-Up (Awareness & Attraction)

It’s a weekday morning. Jordan is scrolling through Instagram during his coffee break at work.

Morning — Sponsored Post

A sponsored post from PureGym pops up: “Meet Zara from Shoreditch — she joined PureGym last year and found her 6AM motivation.” (He thinks: “At least they’re not using fake models for once. If it’s not too crowded, I might actually go after work.”)

Commute — Mind the Fitness Gap

Later, during his commute, the "Mind the Fitness Gap" escalator moment kicks in — the stairs slow down, and the digital ads along the sides display the message: "Your glutes will thank you later." He smirks, half-embarrassed, half-intrigued.

Station — Excuse Booth

At the top of the station, he spots an “Excuse Booth”, styled like a classic red London phone box but repainted PureGym blue. A sign reads: “Step in. Say your excuse. We’ll tell you the truth.” He hears someone inside laughing as a voice says, “Excuse rejected.”

Walk to Work — Gym Finder Stickers

On his walk to work, Gym Finder Stickers guide his path: “10 squats north = PureGym Shoreditch.” By lunchtime, PureGym’s name has popped up three different ways, enough to lodge itself in his brain.
